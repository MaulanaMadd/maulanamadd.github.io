About Us

Profile

Leading Plastic Fabricators in the North West

Established in 2000, Atlas Plastic started out as a small workshop, catering for a handful of industrial clients in our local region. In the 20+ years since, our company has grown from those humble beginnings into a nationally renowned provider of plastic products.

While we continue to work with local firms, our client base has expanded steadily over the years as we deliver our exceptional products to multinational blue-chip companies on a weekly basis. Our commitment to our customers has never wavered over all that time. To this day, whether you’re a sole trader or a global conglomerate, you can expect the same high standard of work from our team of plastic fabricators, designers, and installers.

As the North West’s leading plastic fabricators, we are constantly innovating new processes and improving our existing ones to make sure our customers get the products they deserve. We have the tools, skills, and experience to produce stunning one-of-a-kind installations in durable plastic as well as more standardised products, skilfully tailored to meet your branding needs, and available in bulk for company-wide rollouts.
